Project 2: Exquisite Corpse


An Exquisite Corpse by Brian Wissing and Jin Park

Our Rules:
Subject matter is limited only by imagination
Could be an image, sketch, or graphic made on Photoshop/Illustrator
Brian's additions must have a bright theme
Jin's additions must have a dark theme
3x3 square with 9 separate parts
Brian begins on the top left part, Jin follows with the top middle part, and both alternate in a clockwise spiral direction.
